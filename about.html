The Vision

Putting Money in the Pockets of South Africans

RoadReach Media was founded by Mrs Fulufhelo Tambani (Fulu) with a vision to give South African businesses a louder voice on the streets. She believes that the most effective advertising happens where life happens — in our communities.

The idea struck during a morning school run. Watching a branded car pass by, she asked: "What if I created a company that paid everyday South Africans for brands advertising on their cars?"

That thought never gave her peace. It grew into a vision, a plan, and finally — RoadReach. Today, we are South Africa's premier mobile billboard network, helping brands connect with communities while putting money directly into the pockets of ordinary South Africans.
