RoadReach Launches: A New Chapter for South African Advertising

By Fulufhelo Tambani, Founder & Director

Today marks a milestone that began as a simple thought during a morning school run. Watching a branded car pass by, I asked myself: "What if I created a company that paid everyday South Africans for brands advertising on their cars?"

That question never gave me peace. It grew into a vision, a plan, and finally — a company. Today, I am proud to announce the official launch of RoadReach Media.

Our Mission

RoadReach exists to bridge two fundamental needs: the need for brands to connect authentically with communities, and the need for everyday South Africans to earn additional income in a challenging economy.

Our model is simple but powerful. Brands pay for premium vehicle advertising space. Drivers earn money by displaying those ads on their cars. South Africa's roads become a dynamic, hyper-local media network that puts money into the pockets of ordinary people.

"My mission is simple: to put money in the pockets of South Africans like me. I am inviting every fellow South African who owns a car to join me on this people-centred mission."
— Fulufhelo Tambani, Founder

What We've Built

As of launch day, RoadReach has already recruited over 750 drivers across South Africa and partnered with leading signage experts including Signorama to ensure every vehicle wrap meets the highest quality standards.

Our fleet spans all vehicle categories — from compact city cars to SUVs and bakkies — giving brands the flexibility to choose the right canvas for their message.

Our Commitment

We are committed to three core principles:

  • Transparency: Every campaign is tracked and reported with full data transparency. Brands know exactly where their ads travelled and what impact they generated.
  • Quality: We work with industry-leading installation partners to ensure every wrap looks premium and professional — protecting both the brand and the driver's vehicle.
  • Impact: 70% of every media Rand goes directly to drivers. We're not just advertising — we're empowering communities.

This is just the beginning. We have ambitious plans to grow our fleet, expand into new regions, and help South African brands of all sizes achieve unmatched visibility on the streets that matter most.
