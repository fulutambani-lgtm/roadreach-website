Simple Process

How It Works

Becoming a RoadReach driver is straightforward. We handle the wrap installation, you drive as normal, and you earn a monthly payout.

1

Submit Your Expression of Interest

Send us your details including your vehicle type and size. We'll review your application and let you know if your vehicle meets our quality standards.

2

Vehicle Assessment

We'll inspect your vehicle to confirm it's in good condition and measure it to determine the right wrap package and your monthly payout.

3

Professional Wrap Installation

Our signage partners (Signorama) install a premium-quality vinyl wrap on your vehicle at no cost to you. No damage to your paint — guaranteed.

4

Drive & Earn

Drive your normal routes — commuting, errands, or business travel. Your monthly payout is deposited directly into your bank account.

Requirements

What You Need to Qualify

SA Citizen or Resident

Must be a South African citizen or permanent resident with a valid ID document.

Valid Driver's Licence

Hold a valid South African driver's licence and be at least 18 years of age.

Own a Vehicle

Own a vehicle in good condition that meets our quality standards. All sizes welcome — from hatchbacks to minibuses.

Drive Regularly

Use your vehicle at least 5 days per week so the brand gets adequate exposure. Your normal daily routine is usually sufficient.

Smartphone

Have a smartphone to receive campaign updates, submit periodic photos if required, and stay in touch with the RoadReach team.

Bank Account

Have a South African bank account for monthly payout deposits.

FAQ

Application Questions

Common questions about the driver application process.

How long does the application process take?

Once applications reopen, the process typically takes 1-2 weeks from submission to wrap installation, depending on vehicle inspection scheduling and wrap availability.

Is there a cost to join?

No. There is no cost to join RoadReach. We cover the full cost of professional wrap installation and removal. Your vehicle just needs to pass our quality inspection.

What if my car has existing damage or rust?

Vehicles must be in good condition with no significant rust, damaged panels, or peeling paint. Minor wear and tear is generally acceptable. We'll assess your vehicle during the inspection.

Can I remove the wrap if I sell my car?

Yes. We'll arrange professional removal at no cost to you. You're free to leave the program after the initial campaign commitment period with 30 days' notice.
