FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from potential drivers.

How much can I earn?

Earnings depend on the wrap type displayed — Lite, Standard, or Premium. Average earnings start at R2,500 per month and increase with larger wraps. You'll know exactly what you'll earn before agreeing to any campaign.

Will the wrap damage my car's paint?

No. We use premium-quality vinyl wraps from trusted suppliers. When professionally installed and removed, they protect your paint and leave no residue. Many drivers find their paint is in better condition after removal.

How long do I need to display the wrap?

Campaigns typically run for a minimum of 3 months. After that, wraps can be removed or replaced with a new campaign. You're free to leave after the initial commitment period with 30 days' notice.

Do I need to drive a certain amount?

We ask that you drive your vehicle regularly — ideally at least 5 days per week — to ensure the brand gets adequate exposure. Your normal daily routine (commuting, errands, etc.) is usually sufficient.
